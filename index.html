Chapter 01

2021

The Genesis

Biplab Group was founded with a bold vision to reshape West Bengal's industrial landscape. The first manufacturing unit was established, laying the groundwork for diversified operations.

Chapter 02

2022

Paints & Coatings

Berger & Duco Store launched as Bankura's premier paint destination. Authorized dealership established for Berger Paints and Duco auto finishes, bringing premium coatings to West Bengal.

Chapter 03

2023

EV Mobility

Bankura EV Care division was born, bringing electric vehicle sales, servicing, and charging infrastructure under the Biplab umbrella. First EV showroom opened in Bankura town.

Chapter 04

2024

EV Revolution

Entered the electric vehicle mobility sector with smart EV prototypes. Strategic partnerships forged with battery tech providers and urban transport authorities across West Bengal.

Chapter 05

2025

Unified Ecosystem

All six divisions were brought under a single digital command layer. Production data, EV telemetry and coatings QA merged into one intelligent operations stack — powering real-time decisions across every Biplab site.

Chapter 06 — Now

2026

Kinetic Evolution

Full-scale EV production is live. Biplab Group now operates across six core divisions with 100% precision manufacturing, serving clients nationwide and preparing for international expansion into South-East Asia.
