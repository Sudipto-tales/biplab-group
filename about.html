Our Origin

Our Story

Founded in 2021 in the heart of Kolkata, Biplab Group was born from a singular conviction: that West Bengal could become the epicenter of India's next industrial revolution. What began as a bold vision by Biplab Mondal has since grown into a diversified conglomerate that bridges legacy industries with future-forward innovation.

The name "Biplab" means revolution -- and that ethos of kinetic evolution drives everything we do. From our earliest days, we set out to build across sectors that matter: EV mobility, software engineering, smart manufacturing, infrastructure, specialty paints, and deep-tech R&D. Each division operates with precision and purpose, yet together they form an integrated ecosystem designed for scale.

Today, with six operational divisions and a growing team of over 500 professionals, Biplab Group stands as a testament to disciplined execution. We don't chase trends -- we architect systems that endure. Our commitment to sustainability, community development, and technological excellence remains the foundation upon which every decision is made.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To revolutionize industrial operations through precision engineering, sustainable practices, and cutting-edge technology. We are committed to building systems that uplift communities, empower innovation, and deliver measurable impact across every sector we touch.

Our Vision

To become India's most integrated industrial conglomerate, setting new benchmarks in EV mobility, smart manufacturing, and digital transformation. We envision a future where sustainable industry and technological innovation converge to redefine what is possible.

The Path Forward

Our Journey

2021

Genesis

Biplab Group was incorporated in Kolkata with a vision to build an integrated industrial conglomerate. The first manufacturing unit was established, laying the foundation for what would become a multi-sector powerhouse.

2022

Industrial Expansion

Launched Smart Manufacturing and Luxe House Paints divisions. Expanded operations to multiple production facilities across West Bengal, creating over 200 jobs in the region.

2023

Digital Frontier

The Kolkata Coder, our software and digital solutions division, went live. Introduced AI-driven quality control systems across manufacturing units and began R&D Lab operations.

2024

EV Revolution

Entered the electric vehicle mobility sector with an ambitious lineup of commercial and personal EV solutions. Established strategic partnerships with battery technology providers and charging infrastructure networks.

2025

Unified Ecosystem

All six divisions were unified under a single digital platform for resource optimization and cross-sector synergy. Crossed the 500-member team milestone and expanded Smart Infrastructure operations.

2026

Kinetic Evolution

The year of kinetic evolution -- scaling our EV portfolio nationally, launching next-gen paint formulations, and unveiling our proprietary manufacturing OS. Biplab Group is now positioned as one of Eastern India's fastest-growing industrial conglomerates.
