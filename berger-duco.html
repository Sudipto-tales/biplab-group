Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What brands do you stock?

We are authorized dealers for Berger Paints, Asian Paints, and their sub-brands including Duco, Berger Silk, Berger Homeshield, and Asian Paints Royale.

Do you offer home painting services?

Yes! We provide end-to-end painting services including wall preparation, primer application, and two-coat finish with premium paints. Free site inspection included.

Can I become a paint dealer through Biplab Group?

Absolutely. We onboard sub-dealers across West Bengal. Visit our Community page or contact us directly for dealership opportunities, territory mapping, and margins.

How does the Color Mixer work?

Pick any two colors from our on-screen artist palette, click Mix, and we'll generate the exact HEX code. Submit a request and our team will custom-mix that shade in-store for you.

Do you provide automotive Duco finishes?

Yes, we carry the full Asian Paints Duco line and PU finishes for automotive refinishing. We serve body shops, garages, and individual vehicle owners with professional-grade products.

What is the delivery range?

We deliver across Bankura district and surrounding areas. Bulk orders get free delivery. For other regions, contact us for shipping estimates.
