FAQ

Frequently asked questions!

1. What services does Stuxen provide?
+

We offer a comprehensive range of digital services including branding, UI/UX design, visual identity, web development, digital marketing strategy, and ongoing creative support for businesses of all sizes.

3. How long does it take to complete a project?
+

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. On average, website projects take 4–8 weeks, while branding and marketing campaigns may take 2–6 weeks depending on deliverables.

5. Can you redesign my existing website?
+

Absolutely. We specialize in both new website builds and redesigns. We start with a thorough audit of your current site to identify opportunities and craft a strategy that improves both design and performance.

7. What makes Stuxen different from other agencies?
+

We combine creativity with strategy. Every project is treated as a unique challenge — we don't use cookie-cutter templates. Our team deeply invests in understanding your brand and audience to deliver genuinely distinctive results.

9. Do you work with clients internationally?
+

Yes, we work with clients globally. Our team is fully remote-capable and experienced in managing projects across different time zones and cultures seamlessly.

2. How much does a typical project cost?
+

Pricing depends on the scope, complexity, and timeline of the project. We offer flexible packages for startups as well as enterprise-level solutions. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your needs.

4. Do you work with small businesses?
+

Yes! We love working with businesses of all sizes, from ambitious startups to established enterprises. We have packages and processes designed to scale with your needs and budget.

6. Do you provide ongoing support after completion?
+

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to ensure your digital assets continue to perform at their best. We're here for you long after the initial launch.

8. How do I get started with Stuxen?
+

Simply reach out via our contact page or email us directly. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your goals and put together a proposal tailored to your project within a few days.

10. Can you customize services based on my needs?
+

Absolutely. We understand that no two businesses are alike. We work closely with you to create a custom service package that addresses your specific challenges and objectives.
